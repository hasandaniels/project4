The Benefits of Paying a Student Athlete

There are many benefits that come with paying student athletes!

The first benefit of paying student athletes is when you pay them, they will probably give the sport 100% effort and that will lead to the team having a good chance of winning a national championship.

The second benefit is student athletes can learn how to manage their money. This will be a lesson outside of the things you learn in the class room and the students athletes place of play.

The third benefit is it will teach student athletes the lesson of having to go out and earn your keeps. Basically you have to work hard for what you want and things wont be handed to you.
