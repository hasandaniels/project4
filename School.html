School Time Affected

Student athletes have many practices and games which leads to a busy life.

Being a student athlete myself and having to play three sports a year, I had to miss a lot of class time due to the fact that I had many away games. Being said college athletes have a lot of away games,therefore they are always traveling,and in some scenarios they are going out of state

According to bussinessoutsider.com,"In the study, Division I men’s basketball players reported spending 34 hours per week on athletics. FBS football players reported spending 42 hours per week on athletic activities—a full-time job."

Student athletes are basically balancing a full time job with school and yet they are not payed I dont see the justice in that.
