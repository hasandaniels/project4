The Struggles A College Student Athletes Endures

Being a student athlete is not a walk in the park. Student athletes have a lot on their hands and soemtimes it can be overwhelming and through the proccess oh having

The first of many struggles is having to balance sports with your academic life. Being a student is a hard enough job but having to attend classes,then study for test, and complete projects makes the job of a student athlete even harder.

The second of many struggles is getting used to traveling to many different states. Not only are you missing class time, but you are not at the place you consider "home away from home" so college student athletes might get home sick.

The third of many struggles is the relationship student athletes have with other people outside of the sports world. With all the school, practices, and then studying, student athletes dont have much time for interacting with other people.
